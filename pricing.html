How Pricing Works

DocMersion pricing is based on the number of pages and documents you need to convert and manage. Each plan includes all essential features for compliance and accessibility. For organizations with unique needs, our Enterprise plan offers custom solutions and integrations.

Why DocMersion for Large PDFs?

  • Turn massive, hard-to-navigate PDFs into beautiful, organized web pages
  • Perfect for policies, handbooks, and compliance documents
  • Give your community instant, searchable access to critical information
  • Ensure full WCAG 2.1 compliance and modern translation options

Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a page?

A page is a single PDF page or content page converted into a digital, searchable web page.

Can I upgrade or downgrade plans?

Yes! You can change your plan at any time to fit your needs. Enterprise plans are fully custom.

Is my data secure and compliant?

Absolutely. DocMersion is built for compliance and security, including WCAG 2.1, ADA, and CIPA standards.
