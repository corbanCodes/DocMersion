Compliance: WCAG 2.1, ADA, & CIPA

Our Commitment to Accessibility & Compliance

At DocMersion, we believe that digital accessibility and regulatory compliance are not just legal requirements—they’re essential to serving all people fairly, building trust, and fostering innovation.

  • WCAG 2.1: We adhere to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 to ensure our platform is usable by everyone, including people with disabilities.
  • ADA: Our site and platform are designed to meet standards set by the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA), supporting equal access and opportunity for all users.
  • CIPA: We support compliance with the Children’s Internet Protection Act (CIPA), helping organizations provide safe, responsible digital environments.

Why does this matter?

  • Accessibility opens your content to everyone, including people with disabilities.
  • Compliance reduces legal risk and demonstrates social responsibility.
  • Accessible and compliant sites are easier to use, rank better in search engines, and serve broader audiences.

We are committed to ongoing improvements and welcome feedback to make our platform even more accessible and inclusive. If you have questions about compliance, accessibility, or best practices, please contact us.

What is Digital Compliance?

Digital compliance means ensuring your website and digital documents meet legal and ethical standards for accessibility, privacy, and security. For public-facing organizations, this is not just best practice—it’s the law.

Key Standards We Meet

  • WCAG 2.1: Web Content Accessibility Guidelines ensure content is usable by people with disabilities.
  • ADA: The Americans with Disabilities Act mandates digital accessibility for equal opportunity.
  • CIPA: Children’s Internet Protection Act requires safe and responsible online environments in schools and libraries.

Risks of Non-Compliance

  • Legal action and costly lawsuits
  • Loss of funding or grants
  • Damaged reputation and public trust
  • Excluding people who need your information most

How DocMersion Ensures Compliance

  • Automated accessibility testing on all documents
  • Manual audits by accessibility experts
  • Continuous updates to meet evolving standards
  • Welcoming user feedback to improve accessibility

Continuous Education & Resources

We provide ongoing education for our team and clients, and curate resources to help you stay informed. Visit the W3C Web Accessibility Initiative for more, or contact us for compliance support.

Global Accessibility Laws & Standards

DocMersion is designed to help organizations meet not just U.S. standards, but also international accessibility requirements such as EN 301 549 (EU), AODA (Canada), and more. We stay informed about global regulations to help you serve a worldwide audience.

Data Privacy & Confidentiality

Compliance isn’t just about accessibility—it’s about protecting your users’ information. We follow best practices for data privacy and confidentiality, supporting regulations such as GDPR (Europe), FERPA (U.S. education), and more, so your documents and your users are always protected.

Inclusive Design & User Feedback

We believe compliance is a journey, not a checkbox. Our team works with real users—including those with disabilities—to test, refine, and improve our platform. Your feedback is always welcome and helps us build a more inclusive digital world.
